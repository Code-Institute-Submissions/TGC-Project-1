Moshe Feldenkrais
(1904-1984)

The Feldenkrais Method was developed by Dr. Moshe Feldenkrais.

It teaches unity of mental and muscular through the body, allowing awareness, re-alignment and re-connection.

Resulting in relief from involuntary contractions or limitations.

What We Provide
